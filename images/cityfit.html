Health Hub
CityFit is a brand by Fairfax Media, that attempts to take all its health and fitness content and events and put it into one hub. This includes consolidating The Juice Daily, along with events like City2Surf and have them sit under CityFit. I was tasked to create the branding and logos, look and feel, website design and email design. This included new concepts for the City2Surf logo as well.
