The Juice Daily
The Juice Daily is an amazingly helpful health website that will help you navigate through the many health myths found online. Originally planned as a small blog to help market Bodypass, The Juice Daily has grown from being a side project to being featured as SMH.com's go-to for health and wellness.
Branding & UI design
The Juice Daily was conceived one afternoon whilst chatting with my Strategy Director and Health Editor. We wanted a way to market one of our health products (Bodypass) as well as bring relevant health articles to the Australian public. It was decided to go ahead and build the blog, provided we could do it with no budget and no time allocated. Somehow, we succeeded.

I worked closely with the Health Editor to ensure her vision was kept in-tact. The design is heavy on blacks, whites and thin lines as the brief described. All the UX design was done by our in house UX guru.
